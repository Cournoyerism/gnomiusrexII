Edictum Perpetuum  ·  Anno Domini Thou Hast Been Gnomed By Decree of the Ancient Ceramic Order

This ancient ceramic guardian has chosen thy lawn above all others in the parish. His watch has begun, and the weight of his gaze shall not leave thee until thy sacred duty has been carried out in full.

Be it known that thee has been granted six hundred and four thousand, eight hundred seconds — exactly one week — to carry the Gnome to an unsuspecting home, decorate and enhance the Gnome before placing him upon their lawn, and pass along this very notice so that the ancient order may continue unbroken.

Only once thy duty is fulfilled shall thee receive five fleeting seconds of pure internal gratification — warm, real, and gone before thee knows it — before it fades, as all good things do, into the quiet numbness of everyday life.

Do not ignore the Gnome. The Gnome sees thee.
